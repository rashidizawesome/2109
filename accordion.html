Fingerboarding is what you used to do with tech decks in elementary school. However, the fingerboarding community use higher-grade materials such as wood for decks, metal for trucks, and real urethane for wheels instead of plastic. Various brands make handmade replicas of skateboarding parts using identical materials. In short, Fingerboarding is a finger-sized simulation of real skateboarding!

Well, this is a common one! In fact, there are PROFESSIONAL fingerboarders! Crazy right? Fingerboarding is an activity done by people all across the world and all across the spectrum of age. Fingerboarding is inside of everyone!

My name is Rashid Haroun, an 11th grader from Seattle, Washington. I am a fingerboarder in my free time because the thought of doing nearly impossible skatebaord tricks with my fingers attracted me. I have been fingerboarding for at least 7 years now!
